What is TI-BOT ?

TI-BOT is an intelligent AI assistant designed to make campus life easier at Tishk International University – Sulaimani. It helps users quickly access essential information such as office locations, staff contacts, service departments, university details, and room navigation—all through a simple conversation. TI-BOT was developed to solve real daily challenges within the university by providing fast, accurate, and friendly support without needing to search or ask multiple people. This project represents a step toward bringing modern technology and innovation into TIUS.
